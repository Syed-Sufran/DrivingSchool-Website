Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I learn if I have never driven? expand_more

Absolutely! Most of our students are complete beginners. Our patient instructors start from scratch, teaching steering, pedals, and gear control on quiet roads first.

How many classes will I need? expand_more

Our standard car course includes 15 to 20 daily sessions. This is usually sufficient for most students to gain confidence and become test-ready.

Do you provide doorstep pickup? expand_more

Yes, doorstep pickup and drop-off are optional benefits for our LMV (Car) and Refresher courses across standard areas near our branches.

Will you help with licence procedures? expand_more

Yes, complete RTO assistance is included. From age proof submission for Learner's Licence to slots booking and documentation for your final Driving Licence test, we guide you.

Do women instructors exist? expand_more

Yes, we have patient, experienced female driving instructors on our team. Please request them when booking your batch!

What documents are required? expand_more

For age and address proof, you can provide your Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Birth Certificate, along with passport-size photographs.

How long does it take to become confident? expand_more

Most learners start driving confidently in traffic around the 10th to 12th session, after mastering basic vehicle controls.
